Goalie Development Camps

TPG is Canada’s choice for goaltender development.

All TPG sessions are run by trained goaltending coaches who have played, or are currently playing or coaching at elite levels. The camps all have a low goalie to coach ratio to ensure ample crease and personal instruction time to each athlete. The most up-to-date technical and tactical elements of the position will be employed with taking into account the stylistic differences and uniqueness of each individual athlete.

Spring/Summer Academy

Every Sundary
April 1 to June 24
5pm or 6:15pm
Multi-day

At Crowfoot Arena

The Sunday night Academy’s are a great way for goaltenders to stay sharp while avoiding burnout. Spring and summer are vital times for goaltenders to make necessary changes and adjustments in their game that they can’t make during the season. Technical and tactical elements of the position will be worked on throughout the academy to ensure you are ready, more skilled, and confident leading into your camps for the upcoming season.
