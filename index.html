Our Services

Year Round Development

Top Prospects Goaltending is a year round goalie program for goaltenders of all skill levels, male or female, children, teens and adults alike. We understand the stylelistic differences in individual athletes and look to enhance each goaltenders specific style.

The program allows goaltenders to improve their skills through the repetition of proper and sound technique in a fun, upbeat environment with instruction from experienced goaltending professionals. Goalies will receive instruction on the most progressive save techniques, with a focus on building strong fundamentals through innovative drills, muscle memory and positive reinforcement

Advantages of TPG

Top Prospects Goaltending always provides a great instructor to goalie ratio to ensure ample crease time for each athlete. We conduct "coach the coaches" seminars as well as conduct evaluation education sessions for minor hockey associations. TPG travels to teams practice facilities to provide specific instruction to goaltenders of all ages, and skill levels. This can be shared by both goalies.

Services

We are continually putting on development camps throughout Calgary and the surrounding areas. To find out when and where camps are being go to our Camps section.

Instructors are available for private 1 on 1 instruction to goaltenders in and around the Calgary area. (Goalie is responsible for providing the ice.)

Minor Hockey Association Support: We conduct independent goaltender evaluations for all minor hockey associations encompassing goaltenders of all age levels. TPG is also available to run yearlong goaltender development programs for minor hockey associations.

Personalized Programs: TPG is available to provide individual strength & conditioning goalie specific programs for both in and off season. TPG is available to provide guided sport imagery training from a professional mental training coach.

On-site Camps: We are available to run camps in your area.
